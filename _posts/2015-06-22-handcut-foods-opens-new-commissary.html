--- layout: post title: HandCut Foods Opens New Commissary Kitchen date: '2015-06-22T11:36:00.000-07:00' author: Waechter Architects tags: modified_time: '2015-09-29T11:47:59.064-07:00' thumbnail: http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-HAOysXsMe2s/VgrZ8P1a1QI/AAAAAAAAAdI/g550ftmgLlY/s72-c/HandCut_1.JPG blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-9105622035626760129.post-7629166895152194186 blogger_orig_url: https://waechterarchblog.blogspot.com/2015/06/handcut-foods-opens-new-commissary.html --- HandCut Foods is a local catering company that creates fresh, healthy meals to K-12 schools and private events. Looking to ramp up the scale of their daily production, HandCut Foods contracted WA to design a new cutting-edge commissary kitchen. Located in a re-purposed packing and shipping warehouse off of N. Elston Avenue in Bucktown, the new 5,000 SF space features a custom stainless steel hood over the cooking and baking equipment, a new skylight, mezzanine meeting space, a large walk-in refrigerator and freezer, and dedicated food packaging room.
