Waechter Architects was engaged to extend the lounge area of the Midway Club to include room for a billiards table and additional space for larger events. Much care was taken to mimic the style and finish of the millwork of the existing lounge to ensure the two rooms felt cohesive as one space. The University is currently having a matching wood floor fabricated, which will replace the carpet shown.
